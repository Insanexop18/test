Our Story

Built out of frustration.

SparkForge started when four engineers got tired of watching agencies burn client budgets on slide decks and status meetings. We'd seen too many businesses overpay for mediocre websites built by people who'd never actually shipped a product.

So we started building. We took on our first client in 2022, shipped their site in three weeks, and never looked back. Today we've shipped over 50 sites across e-commerce, agencies, SaaS, and enterprise — each one built with the same care we'd put into our own products.

We're not a freelance collective or a bloated agency. We're four people who genuinely love what we do, and we think that shows in the work.

What Drives Us

We build by a code.

Four principles that shape every project we touch — not words on a wall, but decisions we make daily.

Ship, then improve.

A live site beats a perfect mockup. We move fast, launch early, and iterate with data — not hypotheticals.

Radical transparency.

You'll always know where your project stands. No smoke, no mirrors. We share progress, blockers, and decisions as they happen.

Craft over quantity.

We deliberately take fewer projects so each one gets our full attention. You're not a ticket number — you're the product we're building.

Client success = our success.

We only win when you win. That means honest advice, even when it's not what you want to hear, and solutions that actually serve your goals.

Our Journey

How we got here.

2022

SparkForge is born.

Mann ships the first SparkForge site for a local business out of a shared apartment in Delhi. The team forms organically — engineers tired of the agency circus.

Early 2023

First 10 clients.

Word-of-mouth brings in the first batch of paying clients. SparkForge ships an e-commerce store, two agency websites, and a custom SaaS dashboard — all within 6 months.

Kushal and Ashmeet join as AI/ML engineers. SparkForge begins integrating machine learning into client products — chatbots, recommendation engines, and analytics dashboards.
